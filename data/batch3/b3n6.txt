CoreWeave Signs $11.9 Billion AI Infrastructure Deal with OpenAI

https://www.rcrwireless.com/20250312/featured/coreweave-openai-ai

3/12/2025

CoreWeave secured strategic agreement worth up to $11.9B to provide AI infrastructure to OpenAI. OpenAI invested $350M in CoreWeave stock as part of deal. Microsoft accounts for over 60% of CoreWeave's 2024 revenue.
