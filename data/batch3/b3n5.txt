Google and Hugging Face Form Strategic AI Partnership

https://www.cnbc.com/2023/08/24/google-amazon-nvidia-amd-other-tech-giants-invest-in-hugging-face.html

August 24, 2023

Google invested in Hugging Face's $235M Series D round (valuation: $4.5B) alongside Amazon, Nvidia, AMD, IBM. Google Cloud partnership allows developers to use GCP infrastructure for Hugging Face models and services.
