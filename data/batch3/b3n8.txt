Nvidia and AMD Agree to 15% Revenue-Sharing Deal with US Government on China AI Chip Sales

https://www.foxbusiness.com/technology/nvidia-amd-strike-major-revenue-sharing-deal-us-government-over-china-chip-sales

8/11/2025

Nvidia and AMD struck unprecedented deal to pay US government 15% of AI chip sales revenue to China. Nvidia earned ~$17B from China in FY2024; AMD earned $6.2B (24% of total revenue). Deal allows resumption of H20 chip exports.
