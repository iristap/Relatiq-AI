Meta Joins Databricks' $10 Billion Series J Funding Round at $62B Valuation

https://www.cnbc.com/2025/01/22/meta-backs-databricks-as-the-data-analytics-startup-inches-toward-ipo.html

1/22/2025

Meta invested in Databricks' record $10B Series J round (total raised: $14B), valuing company at $62B. Strategic partnership leverages Meta's Llama open-source LLMs with Databricks' data analytics platform. Companies preparing for 2026 IPO.
