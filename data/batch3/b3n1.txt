Microsoft Invests $10 Billion in OpenAI in Third Phase Partnership

https://www.cnbc.com/2023/01/23/microsoft-announces-multibillion-dollar-investment-in-chatgpt-maker-openai.html

January 23, 2023, 2:06 PM UTC

Microsoft announced $10B investment in OpenAI (total $13B cumulative), making Azure OpenAI's exclusive cloud provider. Partnership aims to integrate ChatGPT into Microsoft products including Bing, Office 365, and Azure services.
