Microsoft and OpenAI Restructure Partnership - Microsoft Gets 27% Stake

https://finance.yahoo.com/news/microsoft-holds-27-openai-revamped-213659056.html

October 2025 (revamp announced)

Microsoft and OpenAI overhauled partnership giving Microsoft ~27% equity stake (valued ~$135B). OpenAI committed to $250B Azure cloud purchases through 2032. Deal provides OpenAI more flexibility with third-party partnerships while maintaining Microsoft exclusivity until 2032.
